# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Project Name:** PT Mahaga Widya Cita Corporate Website & Portal
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** July 2026

## 1. Executive Summary
PT Mahaga Widya Cita is an Indonesian company offering various educational, consulting, and software services. This project aims to build a modern, high-performance, and visually stunning corporate website that serves as both a company profile and a portal for its core services.

## 2. Business Objectives
- Establish a strong, professional online presence that builds trust with clients and partners.
- Serve as a central hub for all services: Consulting, Executive Education, Software Services, Governance Reviews, Online Courses, and Digital Conferences.
- Facilitate lead generation via contact forms and newsletter subscriptions.
- Provide a platform for publishing company insights, articles, and policy reviews.

## 3. Target Audience
- Corporate clients seeking consulting and software services.
- Professionals looking for executive education and certification.
- General public and students interested in online courses and webinars.
- Partners and stakeholders.

## 4. Key Features & Requirements

### 4.1. Core Pages (Frontend)
- **Home Page:** Hero section, About Us snippet, Services overview, Latest Articles, Newsletter Signup.
- **Tentang Kami (About Us):** Company profile, Management team, Experts, CEO message.
- **Layanan (Services):** Detailed pages for each service offering.
- **Mitra & Karir:** Partners list and job openings.
- **Kontak:** Contact form, map, and social links.

### 4.2. Content Management System (CMS)
- **Articles & Blog:** Ability to create, edit, and categorize articles.
- **Policy Reviews:** Dedicated section for publishing in-depth policy analyses.
- **User Management:** Admin and Member roles.
- **Media Management:** Easy upload and management of images and documents.

### 4.3. UI/UX Requirements
- **Design Aesthetic:** Premium, modern, clean, with vibrant colors (Primary Blue: #1E6FD9).
- **Responsive Design:** Must work flawlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- **Micro-animations:** Smooth scrolling, hover effects, and transitions to feel "alive".
- **Accessibility & SEO:** Semantic HTML, fast load times, and meta tags for search engine optimization.

## 5. Future Phases (Out of Scope for v1)
- E-commerce integration for purchasing online courses directly.
- Complex LMS (Learning Management System) for hosting video content.
